Rules of the Game "Crossy Chicken Time"

Welcome to "Crossy Chicken Time," a fun and fast-paced game where you control a chicken across various challenges! Navigate through obstacles, collect rewards, and master the art of timing in this exciting adventure. Are you ready to become the ultimate chicken champion?

How to Play

The game features three unique modes, each with its own set of challenges. Here’s how it works:

  • MoveGame: Tap the screen to move the chicken forward. Avoid obstacles and collect coins to increase your score.
  • JumpGame: Tap the screen to make the chicken jump over obstacles. Timing is crucial to avoid collisions and keep the chicken safe.
  • RunGame: Swipe left or right to move the chicken across lanes. Dodge incoming obstacles and collect power-ups to boost your score.

Multiplier System

Collect special items to activate the multiplier system, which increases your score exponentially. The longer you survive, the higher your multiplier becomes!

  • X2 Multiplier: Double your score for a limited time.
  • X3 Multiplier: Triple your score for an even greater challenge.
  • X5 Multiplier: The ultimate multiplier for the most skilled players.

Settings

Customize your gaming experience with the following settings:

  • Sound: Toggle sound effects and background music on or off.
  • Controls: Adjust the sensitivity of taps and swipes to suit your play style.
  • Graphics: Choose between different visual themes to personalize your game.
